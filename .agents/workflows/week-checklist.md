description: Shows checklist for one or multiple project weeks, asks which items to start, then implements step by step with confirmation before moving to next item.
---

I am starting a new work session on the project.

Steps to follow:
1. Ask me which week(s) I am currently working on — I may be doing multiple weeks in one session
2. Show me the full checklist for all those weeks combined in one list
3. Ask me which item I want to start with
4. Implement that item step by step, explaining what you are doing at each step
5. After each item is complete, mark it done and ask me to confirm before moving to the next one
6. Do not skip items or reorder them without asking me first
7. If I finish all selected weeks early, ask if I want to pull in the next week's checklist
8. At the end of the session summarize what was completed, what remains, and suggest what to tackle next session
